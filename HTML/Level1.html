Adventure Overview

Join the local adventurer's guild and team up with one of its members. Rank up within the guild through completion of hunts.

Meet Aralana

Aralana is the leader of the local adventurer's guild named Hawk's Heroes. She works in collaboration with the Temple of the Far Horizon to keep the city safe. The adventurers in her guild complete hunts posted by those within the city.

Meet Vogal

Vogal is the latest member of Hawk's Heroes. As a stage fighter, he has learned the basics of swordplay. Even though he is just starting out, he will be a valuable ally to the adventurers.
