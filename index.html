Campagin Overview

This campaign will see the adventurers traverse all across Eastern Oerik. Their journey will begin in the Free City of Greyhawk and end in Northern Flanaess. This campaign will take adventurers from level 1 to level 20. Only levels 1-3 are available at the moment, but more is being worked upon. So, check back for updates.

The Free City of Greyhawk

This city will be a central hub for the campaign. It is also where the first 3 adventures take place.
